Portfolio Kejar.id

Project Overview

Kejar Kejar.id is an educational platform designed to support lifelong learning and skills development for individuals of all ages. With a strong emphasis on practical skills and interactive learning, the platform aims to bridge the gap between traditional education and modern career demands. Through a blend of structured courses, mentorship, and real-world projects, Kejar.id empowers users to achieve their personal and professional goals.

Project Details

To build a platform that offers diverse learning resources, interactive content, and real-time support to help users master new skills efficiently.

  • Frontend: React
  • Backend: PHP, Laravel, Node
  • Database: NoSql
